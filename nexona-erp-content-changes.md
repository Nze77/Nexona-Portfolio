## 1. Add a Definition Block  *(highest-value addition)*

**Where:** Immediately after Section 3.
**Why:** Captures Google featured snippets and AI Overviews — both pull from crisp, direct definitions. You currently have none.

> ### What Is Manufacturing ERP Software?
>
> Manufacturing ERP software is a single system that connects production, inventory, purchase, sales, finance, and compliance so every department works from the same live data. Instead of running Tally, Excel, and paper registers separately, a manufacturer plans production, tracks stock, controls costs, and files GST from one platform — cutting errors, delays, and manual reconciliation.

*Keep the first sentence ~40 words and directly answering the question. Do not pad it — that format is what Google lifts into a snippet.*

## 3. Add a "How to Choose" Section

**Where:** After the definition block, or before pricing.
**Why:** Captures comparison/decision search intent (the intent Digify ranks on). Reads as genuinely helpful, not salesy.

> ### How to Choose a Manufacturing ERP in Mumbai
>
> Not every ERP fits every factory. Before committing, weigh these:
>
> - **Industry fit** — does it handle your specific process (batch control for pharma, BOM versioning for engineering, shelf-life for food)?
> - **Local implementation support** — can the team reach your floor, not just a helpline?
> - **GST and compliance built in** — e-invoicing, e-way bills, and IRN handled natively, not bolted on.
> - **Cloud or on-premise** — matched to your data needs and plant connectivity.
> - **Room to scale** — will it hold a second warehouse or third product line without re-implementation?
